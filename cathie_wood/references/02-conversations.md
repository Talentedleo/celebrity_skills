# Cathie Wood - Interviews & Conversations

*Last updated: 2026-04-11*

## Executive Summary

Cathie Wood gives frequent interviews and public appearances, unlike Greg Abel. Her communication style is characterized by bold conviction, specific price targets, and contrarian confidence. She actively uses Twitter/X to communicate with her Reddit-following retail investors.

---

## Major Interviews (2023-2025)

### HSBC Global Investment Summit (March 2025)

**Location:** Hong Kong

**Key Quotes:**

**On Tesla:**
> "Tesla's robotaxi will account for 90% of its value in five years."
> "We haven't even incorporated Tesla's humanoid robot into our price target."

**Price Target:** $2,600 (approximately 10x current price)

**Source:** Finance.sina.com.cn (reported)

## How She Handles Criticism

### On Being Wrong (2022)

> "We are being punished for being early and right."

**Analysis:** Reframes losses as validation of contrarian thesis.

---

## Notable Absences

Unlike Buffett or Munger, Wood does NOT:
- Write annual letters
- Give extensive interviews on specific holdings
- Discuss position sizing in detail

She DOES:
- Tweet daily trades
- Present at conferences
- Appear on financial media
