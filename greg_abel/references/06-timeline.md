# Greg Abel - Timeline

*(Last updated: 2026-04-10)*

## Early Life & Education

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| June 1, 1962 | Born in Edmonton, Alberta, Canada |
| 1984 | Graduated from University of Alberta with Bachelor of Commerce in Accounting |
| Early career | Worked as chartered accountant at PricewaterhouseCoopers, San Francisco office |
| 1992 | Joined CalEnergy (geothermal electricity producer) |

---

## Career at CalEnergy / MidAmerican

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| Mid-1990s | Moved to U.K. to handle CalEnergy's takeover of British utility — credited as start of transition to Berkshire leadership |
| 1999 | CalEnergy acquired MidAmerican Energy; Berkshire Hathaway acquired controlling interest later that year |
| 1999 | Became president of MidAmerican Energy by time of Berkshire acquisition |
| 2008 | Appointed CEO of MidAmerican Energy |

---

## Rise Within Berkshire

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| 2014 | MidAmerican renamed Berkshire Hathaway Energy (BHE) |
| January 2018 | Named Vice Chairman of Berkshire Hathaway (non-insurance operations); appointed to board of directors |
| 2018 | Received Horatio Alger Award |
| August 2022 | Sold 1% stake in BHE for $870 million |
| October 2022 | Purchased $68 million in Berkshire Class A shares (168 shares at ~$404,761 each) |

---

## Succession to CEO

| Year | Event |
|------|-------|
| May 2021 | Warren Buffett publicly confirms Abel as his successor |
| May 2024 | Buffett reaffirms Abel will have full authority for investing decisions as CEO |
| May 3, 2025 | Buffett announces retirement at year-end; board votes unanimously for Abel |
| January 1, 2026 | Abel becomes President and CEO of Berkshire Hathaway |
